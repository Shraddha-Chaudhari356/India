Khichdi, a simple and nutritious dish made with rice and lentils, is widely regarded as the national food of India. It has been a part of Indian cuisine for centuries and is cherished across the country in different regional variations.

In 2017, India showcased Khichdi as the "Brand India Food" at the World Food India event, highlighting its significance as a unifying dish enjoyed by all, regardless of culture, region, or background. Easy to digest and full of nutrients, it is a comfort food for millions.
