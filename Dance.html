Classical Dances of India

Bharatanatyam

Originated in Tamil Nadu, known for its grace, expressions, and sculpturesque poses.

Kathakali

From Kerala, characterized by colorful makeup, costumes, and storytelling dance drama.

Kathak

Originated in North India, known for spins, intricate footwork, and expressive gestures.

Folk Dances of India

Bhangra

A high-energy dance from Punjab performed during harvest and festivals.

Garba

Popular in Gujarat, performed in circles during Navratri with rhythmic claps and twirls.

Ghoomar

A graceful Rajasthani dance performed by women in colorful swirling skirts.
