Recognized Official Languages

Hindi

The most spoken language in India, primarily in the north.

English

Bengali

Spoken predominantly in West Bengal and Tripura.

Telugu

Spoken widely in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.

Marathi

Official language of Maharashtra.

Tamil

Classical language spoken in Tamil Nadu and Puducherry.

Gujarati

Mainly spoken in Gujarat and nearby regions.

Punjabi

Predominantly spoken in Punjab.
