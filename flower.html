The Lotus is the National Flower of India. It symbolizes purity, divinity, and spiritual enlightenment. The lotus is often associated with Indian mythology, especially in Hinduism and Buddhism.

Despite growing in muddy waters, the lotus remains clean and beautiful, representing resilience and inner strength. It is often seen in ancient Indian art, architecture, and scriptures, reinforcing its importance in Indian heritage.
