The Royal Bengal Tiger is the national animal of India. It represents strength, agility, grace, and power. Found primarily in the Indian subcontinent, it is a majestic and endangered species, playing a crucial role in maintaining the ecological balance.

India is home to more than 70% of the world's tiger population, and the government has taken significant steps for its protection through initiatives like Project Tiger. The Royal Bengal Tiger is admired for its beauty, fierceness, and bravery.
